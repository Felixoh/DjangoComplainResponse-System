Our Location

Kisii County is one of the forty seven Counties of Kenya. It shares common borders with Nyamira County to the North East, Narok County to the South and Homabay and Migori Counties to the West.The County lies between latitude 00 30’ and 100 South and longitude 340 38’ and 350 East.It covers an area of 1,302km2, a population of 1,152,282 people as per the 2009 census.
